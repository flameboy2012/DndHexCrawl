_Landmark / Witch's Dwelling_

A hunched, waterlogged shack pressed against the eastern edge of the grasslands where the river runs fast and loud. The hovel belongs to Mother Dendra, an ancient and unsettling woman who has lived on this bank longer than anyone in Naywich can remember. Bundles of crow feathers and animal bones hang from the eaves, clattering in the river wind.

Locals avoid her but quietly seek her out when desperate — she trades in cures, curses, and uncomfortable truths. She is frequently seen walking the path north to the Crow's Barrow at dusk, and it is widely suspected she is responsible for the mysterious excavation of the mound. What she is looking for — or has already found — is unknown.

The river here is too fast and deep to cross safely. Mother Dendra knows of a crossing further north but shares the information only at a price.
