
_Wilderness Hex / Biome Transition_ A flat expanse where grassland gives way to patchy scrub and pale sandy earth. The air here is warmer than it should be. A small cairn of stacked stones marks what appears to be an old campsite — the ashes are cold but the site is used regularly by someone. The pyramid is clearly visible from here on any day without fog, its silhouette unmistakable against the southern sky.
