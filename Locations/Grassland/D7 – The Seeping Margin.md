
_Wilderness Hex / Biome Transition / Hazard_ The earth here is soft and waterlogged, the grass giving way to reeds and dark mud. The smell of the swamp drifts northward on still days. Twisted willows have begun to take root, their roots drinking deep from water that has no obvious source. Travellers passing through risk becoming mired. At night, faint lights drift above the mud — whether will-o'-wisps or something else, none who have followed them have reported back.
