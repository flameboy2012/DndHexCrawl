_Minor Location / Estate_ A large apple and pear orchard adjacent to Naywich, technically belonging to Lady Hallowstride's estate but leased to a family of orchardists (the Brenfields). The trees are old and gnarled. Locals whisper the orchard blooms unnaturally late in the year—long past it should.
